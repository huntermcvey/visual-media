My opinion

Dr. Evia is probably the most unique teacher I have. He has the ability to incorporate his personality into his teaching, and I do not experience that with many of my other classes. This class has allowed me to use some of my prior knowledge of technology and software, and build on it with every project we do. Our class is based on four modules, every module being something new. I love the way this class is structured and would recommend it to anyone looking to expand their knowledge.

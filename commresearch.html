Class description

This course exposes students to foundational qualitative and quantitative research methods, and enables students to gain basic proficiency in them. Students complete a range of assignments designed to prepare them for their remaining undergraduate coursework, and provide them with skills that are relevant to employers in the field.

My opinion

Professor Haenschen has a very traditional style class. We take all notes by hand, and do not use technology unless the assignment specially requires it. With that being said, I focus best in this class. Someone about writing all the notes down by hand helps information stick in my brain. I have learned various qaulitative and quantitaive sampling methods, and been able to practice them in the field. This class has given me a lot of hands on experience, and information that will be useful with my future jobs.
